# Resources

Continuing your Arduino journey? Here are some excellent resources curated by the IEEE student branch to help you expand your knowledge and tackle more complex projects.

## Official Documentation
- **[Arduino Reference](https://www.arduino.cc/reference/en/):** The complete reference for all built-in functions, variables, and structure of the Arduino language.
- **[Arduino Tutorials (Built-in Examples)](https://docs.arduino.cc/built-in-examples/):** Step-by-step guides for everything from basic LED blinking to complex sensor networks.

## Learning Links
- **[SparkFun Tutorials](https://learn.sparkfun.com/tutorials):** In-depth guides on electronics concepts, components, and project builds perfectly suited for beginners and beyond.
- **[Adafruit Learning System](https://learn.adafruit.com/):** Thousands of high-quality tutorials covering a massive variety of sensors, LED strips, displays, and microcontrollers.
- **[Instructables - Arduino](https://www.instructables.com/circuits/arduino/projects/):** A great place to find inspiration and user-submitted project guides.
